We are all made of the same stuff.

Different kinds of cells that build our physical form.
But at the heart of every cell is our DNA.

Our DNA makes us who we are. It defines what makes us different.

And four letters make our DNA.

They combine to inform us of our ancestry,
to define our physical traits, our response to drugs
and our risk of disease.

They are our life letters.

When we know our own life letters, we know
ourselves.

We can take control of our medical treatment.
And arm the professionals we trust with the
information they need to look after us better.

Welcome to the future of personalised medical treatment.
